Welcome to 3R Biopharma

3R Biopharma is a full service end to end Pharmacovigilance (PV) solutions provider. We strive to provide our best work in all that we do and this is evident in our impeccable track record with the FDA. Whether operating as your complete PV solution or helping with only a select few services, our commitment to quality is evident.

Our numbers speak for themselves – Nine FDA Inspections with no 483 observations!

As your partner our goal is to not only ensure your company’s compliance but to save you significant costs in the process!

3R’s service offerings Includes:

Medical Information Call Centre (24/7) Amenities
Scientific and Medical Literature Monitoring (MLM)
Individual Case Safety Report (ICSR) Tracking
Processing and Submission (Post Marketing and Clinical Trials)
Signal Detection Services
Aggregate Report Preparation and Submission
Database Hosting Solutions
Data Analysis
Data Mining
